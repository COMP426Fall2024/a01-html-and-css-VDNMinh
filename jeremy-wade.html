LEGENDS OF THE OARFISH

River Monsters - Deep Sea Demon

In this video, Jeremy Wade dives down into the ocean of French Riviera in search of the Oarfish. An incredibly ambitious search as it is, he managed to find 2 live, healthy specimens, an achievement completely unheard of until then.

Since this is just A01 and A01 is never that serious in any COMP classes, I'll simply list some insane stuff Jeremy Wade managed to do that made him my idol.

  • Catching an extremely rare Glyphis Shark while hunting a monster Bull Shark (only 20 were ever seen at this time).
  • Discovering a new species of Arapaima in the Amazon.
  • Letting the world be aware of the monstrosity that is the Goliath Tigerfish.
